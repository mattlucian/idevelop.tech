## Context Section

### Title
Growth Ladder Development

### Content
Installed Dev, QA, and Product "Growth Ladders" for career and performance development. Defined 5 levels (Junior, Mid, Senior, Staff, Principal) with clear competency expectations. Created parallel IC and management tracks enabling career growth without forced management. Published frameworks organization-wide enabling self-assessment and transparent promotion discussions.
